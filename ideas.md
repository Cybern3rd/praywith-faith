<idea>
  **Design Movement**: "Ethereal Minimalism" (inspired by James Turrell & Light Art)
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Light as Material**: Use gradients and blurs to simulate light sources rather than solid colors.
  2. **Breathing Space**: Extreme whitespace to create a sense of pause and reflection.
  3. **Soft Transitions**: No hard lines; everything flows into the next state.
  4. **Focus on Text**: The prayer text is the hero; everything else recedes.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Dawn & Dusk**: A palette shifting from deep indigo/violet (night/contemplation) to soft peach/gold (hope/morning).
  - **Emotional Intent**: To guide the user from the noise of the world into a quiet, sacred digital space.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Central Axis**: Content flows down a central pillar, but with asymmetrical floating elements (light orbs).
  - **Card-less**: Avoid boxed containers; let text float on the atmospheric background.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **"The Halo"**: A subtle, slow-pulsing radial gradient behind the active prayer section.
  - **Glassmorphism**: Navigation and tools use heavy blur (backdrop-filter) to feel like frosted glass.
  - **Floating Action Button**: The "Amen" or "Next" action is always clearly elevated.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Slow & Deliberate**: Animations are slower than standard (500ms-700ms) to encourage slowing down.
  - **Scroll-triggered Fade**: Elements gently rise and fade in as you scroll.

  **Animation**:
  - **Entrance**: Staggered fade-up for text lines.
  - **Background**: Very slow, subtle hue rotation on the background gradients.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: *Cormorant Garamond* (Italic for subtitles) - Elegant, timeless, human.
  - **Body**: *Proxima Nova* or *Geist Sans* - Clean, legible, modern but not cold.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: "Modern Monastic" (inspired by Kinfolk & Japandi)
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Tactile Warmth**: Visuals that suggest paper, linen, or stone textures.
  2. **Structured Serenity**: Grid-based but with organic breaks.
  3. **Earth Tones**: Grounded, natural colors.
  4. **Typographic Hierarchy**: Strong contrast between size and weight to guide reading.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Stone & Parchment**: Warm greys, off-whites, deep forest greens, and terracotta.
  - **Emotional Intent**: To feel grounded, ancient yet modern, and permanent.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Split Screen**: Desktop uses a 50/50 split (image/color on left, text on right).
  - **Editorial**: Looks like a high-end magazine layout.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Vertical Lines**: Thin, elegant vertical dividers.
  - **Serif Display**: Large, beautiful serif numbers for the 5 steps.
  - **Texture Overlays**: Subtle grain over the entire UI.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Page Turning**: Transitions feel like sliding a physical paper.
  - **Snap Scroll**: Sections snap gently into place.

  **Animation**:
  - **Parallax**: Images move slightly slower than text.
  - **Mask Reveal**: Images reveal from behind masks.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: *Playfair Display* - High contrast, editorial.
  - **Body**: *Lora* - A serif for body text to enhance the "book" feel.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: "Celestial Glass" (inspired by iOS 18 & VisionOS)
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Depth & Layers**: Multiple layers of translucency.
  2. **Vibrant Darkness**: Deep backgrounds with bright, neon-like accents.
  3. **Fluidity**: Everything is rounded and fluid.
  4. **Immersive**: Full-screen imagery with UI floating on top.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Nebula**: Deep blacks, electric blues, magentas, and cyans.
  - **Emotional Intent**: To feel vast, cosmic, and connected to something larger.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Floating Islands**: Content lives in rounded, translucent containers.
  - **Bottom Navigation**: Controls anchored at the bottom (mobile-first).

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Glow Borders**: Subtle inner shadows and borders that glow.
  - **Mesh Gradients**: Complex, multi-color gradients.
  - **3D Icons**: Simple 3D-rendered icons for sections.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Spring Physics**: Bouncy, responsive interactions.
  - **Haptic Visuals**: Visual feedback that feels "clicky".

  **Animation**:
  - **Morphing**: Shapes morph into each other.
  - **Blur Transitions**: Backgrounds blur in/out on state changes.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: *SF Pro Display* or *Inter* (Tight tracking) - Modern, digital.
  - **Body**: *SF Pro Text* - Highly legible.
</idea>
